*** 0.1.16 Hotfix Changelogs ***

== Gameplay fixes ==
- Fixed desert scourge quantum tunnelling into another dimension while performing its hungry lunge attack in master mode
- Fixed a bug which allowed you to avoid getting the tar mark debuff from peculiar pot if you exited the pot using the blizzard hat's ability
- Crabulon despawns if you recall out while it's doing its husk drop attack, instead of chasing you back to your spawn to crush you

== Visual/Audio fixes ==
- Fixed the custom dust effects from the toxic blowpipe not rendering properly
- Fixed pontiff's piper trying to summon its squid minion while on the main menu character selection screen, potentially making them fail to load if Calamity is enabled
- Fixed blizzard and sandstorm sprites rendering weirdly in the bestiary
- Fixed blizzard and sandstorm sprite in a bottle vanity not flipping with gravity potion
- Added a sound for the dust devil summoned by the sandstorm sprite

== Localization fixes ==
- Added Portuguese localization for content added in the yoblin patch, alongside fixes to translations for existing content
